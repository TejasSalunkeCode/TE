PLASTINDIA 2026: Empowering the Plastics Industry with 'Bharat Next'

PLASTINDIA 2026, themed 'Bharat Next', is a dynamic platform for exhibitors to showcase cutting-edge innovations in processing machinery, moulds and dies, auxiliary equipment, raw materials, finished products, and more. The 'Bharat Next' vision represents progress across all sectors, driving the plastics industry’s growth while fostering valuable networking opportunities and informing attendees on the latest advancements.The event will also spotlight the industry’s potential for technological transformation. Exhibitors can demonstrate how AI-driven predictive maintenance, automated quality control systems, process optimization, and smart supply chain management can boost efficiency, productivity, and sustainability in plastics manufacturing.

Plastindia Foundation

The driving force behind Plastindia Foundation is its dedicated leadership, guided by an esteemed team committed to advancing the growth and innovation of the plastics industry. At the forefront is President Ravish Kamath, whose vision and strategic direction continue to shape the foundation’s impactful initiatives and global presence.

National Executive Council (NEC)

The National Executive Committee (NEC), appointed by the Office Bearers of the Plastindia Foundation, drives PLASTINDIA 2026 forward. This dedicated team ensures the event’s smooth execution and strategic growth, led by Alok Tibrewala, whose leadership and innovative approach play a key role in shaping its success.
